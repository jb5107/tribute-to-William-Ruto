TRIBUTES TO WILLIAM RUTO

  • EARLY LIFE
  • CAREER
  • ACHIEVEMENT

EARLY LIFE

William Ruto was born on December 21, 1966, in Kamagut, a village near Eldoret, Uasin Gishu County, Kenya. He grew up in a humble background, where his parents were peasant farmers. He worked as a chicken seller to earn money for his education.

CAREER

Ruto is a seasoned Kenyan politician who has served as the Deputy President of Kenya from 2013 to 2022, under the presidency of Uhuru Kenyatta. He was elected as the fifth President of Kenya in the August 2022 general election, defeating his closest rival, Raila Odinga.

Achievements

  • Ruto holds a PhD in Chemistry from the University of Nairobi.
  • He was the Minister for Higher Education from 2008 to 2010.
  • He was the Minister for Agriculture from 2010 to 2013.
  • In 2013, he was elected as the Deputy President of Kenya, serving under Uhuru Kenyatta.
  • In 2022, he was elected as the President of Kenya.
